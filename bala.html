Our Mission

We are a small but focused staffing and recruitment company based in Chennai. We have been in the field for 12 years, and over this period have built a solid reputation in servicing our clients. We specialise in placing staff for the BPO, Call centre, IT and Manufacturing. We also place software programmers, project lead and BDM in our client companies.

We operate across the entire country. We can source staff for you in Delhi, Kolkata, Mumbai, Chennai, Bangalore, Hyderabad and other state capitals as well as tier 2 cities. We have offices in three cities (Chennai, Ahmedabad & Kolkata) and associates in other cities to manage the recruitment process.
